    List

    Taj Mahal, Agra- A symbol of love built by Emperor Shah Jahan.
    Khajuraho Group of Monuments, Madhya Pradesh - Famous for their erotic sculptures.
    Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra - Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves.
    Ellora Caves, Maharashtra - A complex of Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain temples.
    Hampi, Karnataka - Ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.
    Fatehpur Sikri, Uttar Pradesh - A historic city founded by Emperor Akbar.
    Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu - Known for its rock-cut temples and sculptures.
    Red Fort Complex, Delhi - A historic fort in the heart of Delhi.
    Qutb Minar and its Monuments, Delhi - The tallest brick minaret in the world.
    Churches and Convents of Goa - Reflecting Portuguese architectural styles.

    Natural Sites

    Kaziranga National Park, Assam - Home to the one-horned rhinoceros.
    Sundarbans National Park, West Bengal - Famous for its mangrove forests and Bengal tigers.
    Nanda Devi and Valley of Flowers National Parks, Uttarakhand - Known for their scenic beauty and biodiversity.
    Western Ghats - A biodiversity hotspot with numerous endemic species.
    Great Himalayan National Park, Himachal Pradesh - Rich in flora and fauna.

    Mixed Site

    Khangchendzonga National Park, Sikkim - Recognized for both its natural beauty and cultural significance.
